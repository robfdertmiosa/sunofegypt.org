Welcome to sunofegypt.org. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and what options you have to control them. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency regarding the technologies we use to improve your experience with the Sun of Egypt 3 game and our service.

Czym są pliki cookie?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer, tablet, or mobile device when you visit websites. They are used to remember your preferences, logins, and to analyze website traffic, which allows us to deliver content better suited to your interests, including information about Sun of Egypt 3.

What cookies do we use?

On sunofegypt.org, we use the following types of cookies:

  1. a) Essential cookies
    These are crucial for the proper functioning of the website. They enable navigation and the use of its basic features. Without these files, the website may not work correctly.
  2. b) Performance and analytical cookies
    These files allow us to count visits and traffic sources, so we can measure and improve the performance of our website. They help us find out which subpages related to Sun of Egypt 3 are most and least popular and see how visitors move around the site.
  3. c) Functional cookies
    They enable the site to remember your choices (such as your username, language, or region you are in) and provide enhanced, more personalized features.

Cookies and Sun of Egypt 3

By using our site, you may encounter content related to the Sun of Egypt 3 game. Cookies help us present reviews, graphics, and game information in a way optimized for your device and browser.

How to manage cookies?

You have the right to decide whether you want to accept cookies. You can change your browser settings to reject all or some cookies. However, please remember that blocking essential cookies may affect the functionality of sunofegypt.org.

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
